Looking to report an issue?

Whether you are a patient, caregiver or healthcare professional, it is important to report adverse events and product issues for Janssen products.
If you have a medical question/general enquiry on a Janssen product, please visit https://www.janssenmedicalcloud.com/en-us/report-an-adverse-event and choose your location.

This website can be utilized for reporting product quality complaints for any Janssen product. If you are involved in a clinical trial, adverse events should be reported to your coordinating study site.
